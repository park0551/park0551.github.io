About Me

Hello! My name is Taylor Park. I'm mixed Korean-Canadian residing in and attending school in Canada. Incidentally, my legal first name is Jaehee - which is Korean. Taylor is my middle name, which I've unofficially adopted as my first/preferred name since 2nd grade. I love my first name, but at this point everyone already knows me as Taylor. My mom and I moved to Ottawa in the early 2000s and I've lived in and around the Ottawa area ever since.

My main interest in life has always been visual art. I love illustration and recently have become very interested in storytelling through the means of webcomics. In the past two years I've taught myself how to use CSP (a popular artist program for iPad, PC, Mac, etc.) and have made a goal to eventually post my own webcomic online for others to see.

My favourite quote comes from the memoir, Tuesdays with Morrie. Even though this was one of those books we had to read in high school, it easily became one of my favourites. Morrie had many aphorisms about life that helped get me through a time when I was diagnosed with a chronic illness. Even after the darkest part of this ordeal, I appreciate and think about his aphorisms all the time.

“The truth is, once you learn how to die, you learn how to live.” - Morrie Shwartz, Tuesdays with Morrie
